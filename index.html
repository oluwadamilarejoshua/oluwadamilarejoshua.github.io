Hi, welcome to my page!

I am a Master's student and also serve as a Research and Teaching Assistant in the Department of Statistics at the Federal University of Technology, Akure (FUTA), primarily mentored by Professor O. A. Adeoti and Dr. O. O. Ojo. My research interests focus on the applications of time-series analysis and machine learning, particularly in finance and economics.

My work focuses on understanding how financial and economic variables interact and influence the world we live in. I am especially interested in developing new statistical methods that can model these relationships more effectively, by reducing the strict, and limiting, conditions required by existing classical approaches. This will allow deeper and more realistic understanding of the evolving structures in financial and economic systems.
